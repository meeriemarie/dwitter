Usability Test Plan

Test Objective

  • Account Creation and login
  • Posting
  • Viewing a feed of posts
  • Interacting with posts and users

Initial Hypothesis and Questions

Hypotheses:

  • Users will find the account creation process intuitive and easy to complete
  • Users will be able to post and view tweets without confusion
  • The feed layout will effectively display content and be engaging
  • Interactions with tweets and other users (liking, following) will be straightforward

Key Research Questions:

  • Do users understand how to navigate the app?
  • Are there any pain points in the account creation or login process?
  • How easily can users post?
  • Is the feed layout visually appealing and easy to navigate?
  • Are interactions with tweets and users (like, follow) clear and accessible?

Methods

Quantitative Methods:

  • Task Completion Time
  • Frequency (e.g., clicking the wrong button, confusion)
  • System Usability Scale (SUS) score (post-test usability questionnaire)

Quantitative Methods:

  • User Observations
  • Interviews

Test Tasks

Tasks for users:

  • Create a new account.
  • Post something.
  • Find a specific post by “@username”
  • Like a tweet and find the page of you liked posts.
  • Log out and log back in.
  • Change your username

Test Procedure

Participant Recruitment:

  • 4-6 participants from the target demographic

Session:

  • Introduction (5min)
  • Task Execution (15-20min)
    • User Observation
    • TCT
    • Error Rate
  • Debrief and Feedback (5-10min)
    • Interview
    • SUS

Interview Questions

  • How would you describe your overall experience with the app?
  • What did you like the most about the app?
  • What did you find most frustrating or confusing?
  • Were there any parts of the app where you felt stuck or unsure of what to do?
  • How did you find the account creation process?
  • Was it clear how to create a post? If not, what made it unclear?
  • Was it easy to find a specific post?
  • Did the layout of the app make sense?
  • Was the liking of posts clear?
  • Did you expect other ways to interact with posts?
  • Did the app meet your expectations for a social media platform? Why or why not?
  • Are there any features you think are missing or could be improved?
