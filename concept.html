Project Concept

Description

On Dwitter, users can create profiles, explore posts by discovering or searching, and engage with others by liking posts and following people to stay updated on their latest shares. When creating a post, users can include images and text. Profiles are customizable with a personalized username and profile picture.

Use Case

Dwitter is a social media app that is aimed at connecting people and giving them a space to express themselves. Users can follow others and like their posts, creating an interactive space for users.

Target Users

  • Young Adults aged 18-34
  • Active on social media platforms
  • Want to share their thoughts, hobbies, personal updates with a broad or niche audience
  • People promoting a brand, selling products, or services
